Volleyball Drop-In Game Format:

Signup Process: Players click the signup button and choose a position on one of the three available teams. Players pay based on their insurance status: $15.50 for players without insurance or $12 for those with insurance. The $3.50 insurance fee is for our liability insurance to play at girls inc and is only once a year. For more information on ticket prices, see the section below. Team Balancing: Eric (the host) will review the teams prior to the event and make any necessary adjustments to ensure balance. Event Day Schedule: Warm-Up (30 minutes): Includes hitting drills, passing, and general warm-up exercises. Game Play: Teams that arrive on time and are complete will play first. The team that is incomplete or arrives late will act as the referee for the first game. Rotation Rules: The losing team leaves the court and referees the next match. The refereeing team will replace the losing team. After a team plays two consecutive matches, they will rotate off and referee. This rotation continues with one team always refereeing. Adjustments: Eric may make on-the-fly adjustments if the teams appear to be unbalanced during gameplay.

SKILL REQUIREMENT:

⚠️ This event is not for beginners. This event is for experienced players with decent skills. As a reminder, you will certify that you meet the following skill requirements when you register: ⚠️

Required Skills:

  • Passing:
  • Reliable with fewer errors and better control.
  • Hitting:
  • Consistent with power and accuracy. You should be a reliable attacker.
  • Rotations (5-1 & 6-2):
  • You should have a solid understanding of volleyball rotations, rarely make positional errors, and grasp strategic elements of the game.
  • Ball Placement:
  • Start showing consistency and strategic placement in your plays.
  • Blocking:
  • Achieve moderate success and show developing skills.
  • Age Requirement:
  • All players must be 18 years of age or older.

These requirements are crucial to ensure the safety, enjoyment, and competitive balance of our games.

Important:

⚠️ If during the game it becomes apparent that you do not meet these stated requirements, you will be asked to leave without a refund. This policy is in place to maintain the integrity of the event and ensure that all participants have a fair and enjoyable experience. ⚠️

Ticket Options:

  • Game Ticket + Yearly Insurance Bundle:
  • $15.50
  • Returning Player with Insurance Purchased:
  • $12.00.

Ticket Options Explained:

If you have not yet paid the $15.50 yearly insurance fee, you have not fulfilled the insurance requirement to play at Girls Inc. of New Hampshire (Nashua). The insurance fee is a one-time, annual payment. If you've already paid it, you do not need to pay again until the year has passed. If you have not purchased this insurance, please pay $15.50, which includes the required insurance fee.

Important:

  • 24-Hour Payment Window
  • Payments must be received within 24 hours of signing up for the event. A $5 late fee will be added for late payments.
  • Payments are required before the event starts. Selecting a ticket just before the event without immediate payment will incur a $5 late fee.

If you are going to be late, please message the host when you expect to arrive.

Exciting Prizes at Every Event!

🎱 Number Draw: At each event, we’ll randomly draw three ping pong balls, each marked with a number. Every participant's name on the team sign-in sheet has a corresponding number. If your number is drawn, you’ll get the chance to play our Plinko game!

🎰 Plinko Game: Those whose numbers are drawn will drop a chip in our Plinko board to determine their prize. The board has slots for copper, silver, and gold cards, each representing a different points

🥇Gold Card: 3 points

🥈Silver Card: 2 points

🥉Copper Card: 1 point

🥇 Tier 1 Prizes(6+ points): Hoodies (6 points), Backpacks (8 points), Pro Volleyballs (Molten Balls 10 points, Wilson Balls 12 points) Balls are subject to availability

🥈 Tier 2 Prizes (2 points): Water Bottles, Drawstring Bags, T-Shirts, Switch Teams Card: Allows the player to switch to any team they want during the session (expires after two uses, limit one per player). Switch Position Card: Allows the player to switch to any position they want during the session (expires after two uses, limit one per player).

🥉 Tier 3 Prizes(1 point): Sunglasses, Stickers, Jibbitz, No Ref Card: Allows the player to skip reffing duties for an entire session (expires after two uses, limit one per player)

Special Benefits for Middle Players:

  • Middle Draw: The first of the three ping pong balls will only be the middle players. After this draw, the selected middle’s ball is removed, and the remaining middle balls are added back into the pool for the final two draws.
  • Bonus Ball: If the special bonus ball is drawn, all middle players present receive either 1 point or a Tier 1 prize of their choice!

Important Details:

  • Prize Redemption: Points accumulate based on the cards you land on. For example, landing on a gold card earns you 3 points. You can redeem your points for prizes, such as a Backpack (8 points), an Indoor Molten Volleyball (10 points), or a Grass Wilson Volleyball (12 points).
  • Monthly Cap: To keep things fair and exciting, only 2 Tier 1 prizes can be redeemed each month.
  • Cool-Down Period: Once you redeem any prize, you enter a 4-week cool-down period where you can’t accumulate points, giving everyone a fair chance to win.
